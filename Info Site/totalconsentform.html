Welcome to the information page

This is part of a research study on reddit, conducted by researchers at Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology.

In this work, we are develping a norm detection algorithm. As part of the research, we collected 2 weeks public commenting data of some selected users (users who are highly active in r/AskReddit). You are one of this users.

Voluntary use of data

From the collected data, we will create the norm detection algorithm. The collected data is anonymous, username of comment author will be changed by a sample number. We will not publish your reddit username in any phase of the research.
However, you can choose whether we can use the comment data for the research purpose. Please go to the Voluntary Participation tab for more information.

Risks

There is no risk associated with this study concerning you.

Benefits

There may be no personal benefit from your participation in the study, but the knowledge received may be of value to humanity.

Confidentiality

The data captured for this study will not include any identifying information about you beyond reddit username. We will change this username with a sample number before any calculation. The username will not be mentioned in any publication or dissemination of the research data and/or results.

Future Use of Data

In the future, once we have removed all identifiable information from your data, we may use the data for our future research studies, or we may distribute the data to other researchers for their research studies. Sharing of data with other researchers will only be done in such a manner that you will not be identified.

Voluntary Participation

You are free to choose whether we can use the collected comment data or not. Since the data is publicly available, if you don't provide any information, by default, we will consider the selection is Yes.

Please pick an option, and provide your reddit username, we will take action accordingly.
