Welcome to the information page

This is part of a research study on reddit, conducted by researchers at Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology.

In this work, we developed an initial algorithm for detecting special pattern (e.g., social greetings, insulting, provoking) in text. As part of the research, we sent a message to a reddit user when we detect a special pattern by our algorithm. Then, we collected public commenting data for one week of the user to update our algorithm. We selected some users (users who are participating actively in r/AskReddit) for this purpose. You are one of this users.

Voluntary Participation

We will use the collected comments data to update the detection algorithm. The collected data will be anonymous - username of comment author will be changed by a sample number. We will not publish your reddit username in any phase of the research.

However, you can restrict whether we can use the collected comment data of your's or not. Since the data is publicly available, if you don't provide any option within one week, by default, we will consider the selection as Yes.

Please pick an option, and provide your reddit username, we will take action accordingly.

Can we use the comment data for research purpose:




Risks

There is no risk associated with this study concerning you.

Benefits

There may be no personal benefit from your participation in the study, but the knowledge received may be of value to humanity.

Confidentiality

The data captured for this study will not include any identifying information about you beyond reddit username. We will change this username with a sample number before using the data. The username will not be mentioned in any publication or dissemination of the research data and/or results.

Future Use of Data

In the future, once we have removed all identifiable information from your data, we may use the data for our future research studies, or we may distribute the data to other researchers for their research studies. Sharing of data with other researchers will only be done in such a manner that you will not be identified.
