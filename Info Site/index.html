Welcome to the information page

This is part of a research study on reddit, conducted by researchers at Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology.

In this work, we are develping a norm detection algorithm. As part of the research, we collected 2 weeks public commenting data of some selected users (users who are highly active in r/AskReddit). You are one of this users.

Voluntary use of data

From the collected data, we will create the norm detection algorithm. The collected data is anonymous, username of comment author is changed by a sample number. We will not publish your reddit username in any phase of the research.
However, it is upto you whether we can use the collected comment data. Please go to the Voluntary Participation tab for more information.

You may print a copy of this consent form for your records.
